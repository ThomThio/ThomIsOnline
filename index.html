First Principles

Principles guide us in what we do. They help us to boil down the essentials, and get to the heart of what matters. I attribute much my capabilities to this framework, and I appreciate your time in reading this to understand on how I approach challenges.

Balance

Knowing when to speed up, or slow down. When to flex, and when to harden. When the situation calls, can one respond adequately?

Simplicity

Focusing on what matters, and finding the simplest paths. Do all solutions need to be complex?

Harmony

和 or Harmony in Japan. Thinking and steps that work well for everyone. Can what we do be a win-win-win for everyone?

How are these achieved?

Trade-Offs & Complements

What if we could put on a different lens, and see the world differently? Which decisions does more harm than good? Does that change if the situation changes?

Technology as Tools

Machine Learning, Cloud, Automation. Buzz words are just a trend. Tech continuously evolves - what is the best course of action with our eyes on the future?

Systems Thinking

The sum of parts are greater than the whole. A well-designed system can bring about greater synergy. Can we make every component, human and machine, work well together?
